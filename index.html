São Paulo

Brasil

São Paulo is a vibrant city that never sleeps.

Full of amazing food, rich culture, and people from all walks of life. Whether you're into stunning buildings, green parks, or just want to try some of the best foods, there's something for you.

Check out some of the coolest things to do:

SP Architecture

São Paulo is a mix of old and new — from colonial churches to futuristic skyscrapers. Walking through the city, you'll see a patchwork of styles and eras. It's a perfect destination for anyone who loves bold design and urban energy.

Edifício Copan

Designed by Oscar Niemeyer, the Copan building is a modernist icon in the heart of São Paulo. Its unique wave shape and massive scale make it one of the city's most photographed landmarks. A true masterpiece of Brazilian architecture.

Street Art

The city's walls are alive with color. São Paulo is one of the top street art capitals in the world, with murals found in alleys, buildings, and viaducts. Artists use public space to tell stories, share protests, and celebrate culture.

What else?

Beyond its famous landmarks, São Paulo offers an endless variety of things to do. It's one of the most culturally diverse cities in the world — home to amazing restaurants, bookstores, street fairs, concerts, museums, and nightlife for every taste. Like any big city, it's a good idea to research neighborhoods and check reviews before heading out, so you can enjoy everything safely and to the fullest.
